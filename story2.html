JPCLinux: The Journey

2018 (Seoul): NanoTech moderators suggested an emulator at Vercel. I chose the name JPCLinux. Though just teenagers, we started in a lab in April 2018.

2023: Moderators worked 6am to 7pm in Japan. I built the first site on Google Sites, influenced by DistroSea.

2024-2025: After a Svelte app launch in April 2024, we successfully published via LlamaCoder in January 2025.
